PLAYBOOK: Potential Data Exfiltration
Description:
Unexpected outbound traffic from servers to unknown external IP addresses may indicate data exfiltration.

Recommended Actions:
- Identify the source and destination of traffic.
- Check authentication logs for suspicious access.
- Disable compromised credentials.
- Block malicious IPs at the firewall.
- Isolate affected systems if necessary.

Severity:
High
